NEXEN TIRE Supplies Original Equipment Tires
for FCA US LLC
- ‘All New 2019 Ram 1500’ to be equipped with ‘ROADIAN HTX RH5’

NEXEN TIRE has been a supplier of original equipment (OE) tires to Porsche, Volkswagen, Škoda, SEAT, and other global automobile manufacturers. This time, NEXEN TIRE has teamed up with the American auto company, Fiat Chrysler Automobiles(FCA) US LLC.

NEXEN TIRE is supplying original equipment (OE) tires for the all-new 2019 Ram 1500 pickup truck manufactured by FCA US LLC.
The all-new 2019 Ram 1500 becomes the benchmark for durability, technology, efficiency and convenience with features never before offered in a pickup and is built at the Sterling Heights Assembly Plant (SHAP) in Sterling Heights, Michigan.


NEXEN TIRE’s ‘ROADIAN HTX RH5,’ specifically designed for Sports Utility Vehicles, offers an excellent ride experience and great handling by enhancing noise comfort, long lasting tread life and snow performance.
NEXEN TIRE stated that “We are continuously investing in R&D to further improve tire quality and technology. We plan to further expand our OE supply to more automakers and models in the near future.”
NEXEN TIRE has supplied OE tires for a number of FCA US vehicle models, including the Ram 3500 and the Chrysler Pacifica minivan.


We will continue to drive hard in 2018 so that more consumers in the world can ride with us. We welcome your attention and support!
